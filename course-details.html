Objectives

At Mind Spark International Montessori Teacher Training Centre, our core objective is to create a nurturing, innovative, and empowering educational environment that supports both educators and young learners. We strive to deliver internationally aligned Montessori teacher training programs—offering Diploma, Bachelor's, and Master's levels—that combine strong theoretical foundations with hands-on internship experiences. Our goal is to shape confident, creative, and child-centered educators who are equipped to inspire the next generation.

In addition to educator training, we are deeply committed to the all-round development of children through our Daycare, After School Care, and Tuition services for KG to 7th Standard. These programs are designed to provide academic support, emotional care, and personal growth opportunities in a safe and engaging setting. By integrating the core principles of Montessori education with modern teaching strategies, we aim to foster curiosity, independence, and lifelong learning in every child we serve. Ultimately, our objective is to be a trusted, all-in-one center where quality teacher training meets holistic child development—nurturing bright minds and compassionate hearts for a better future.

Eligibility

At Mind Spark International Montessori Teacher Training Centre, we welcome passionate individuals from diverse educational backgrounds who are eager to build a meaningful career in early childhood education.

✅ Eligibility Criteria:

Diploma in Montessori Education:
Applicants must have successfully completed Plus Two (12th grade or equivalent) from a recognized board. This course is ideal for school leavers, homemakers, or anyone interested in starting a teaching career with a strong foundation in Montessori methods.

Bachelor’s Program in Montessori Education:
Open to candidates who have completed their 12th grade or any pre-university course. Graduates in any stream are also eligible. This program is designed for those looking for a comprehensive understanding of Montessori education, blending theory and practice over a longer duration.

Master’s Program in Montessori Education:
Applicants must hold a Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university. This advanced-level course is suited for aspiring senior educators, coordinators, or those looking to specialize deeply in Montessori pedagogy and leadership.
